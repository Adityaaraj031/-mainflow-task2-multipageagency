  Our story

A studio that cares about craft as much as results.

NexaStudio was founded in 2016 by a small team of designers and engineers who were frustrated by agencies that treated websites as commodities. We believed — and still believe — that good software is built through careful thinking, not just fast execution.

We work with startups, scale-ups, and established brands across fintech, health, education, and e-commerce. Our fully remote team spans four time zones, which means we ship faster and stay closer to our clients.
